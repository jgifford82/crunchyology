Welcome to

Crunchyology was born out of one of those classic pregnancy cravings—you know, the kind that makes zero sense where nothing was going to stand between you and that perfect bite. I had to have something crunchy and flavorful. Nothing store-bought was cutting it, so I rolled up my sleeves, raided the pantry, and started making granola in my kitchen.

Turns out, I didn't just satisfy the craving—I fell in love with the whole process. The mixing, the baking, the way the house smelled like toasted oats and magic. I started handing out jars to friends (hello, best edible gift ever). And my boys? They couldn't get enough of it. Watching them devour something I made from scratch was the best feeling.

Fast forward four years, and here we are—Crunchyology is officially out in the wild. I'm now selling my granola at the farmers market and from home, made the same way it started: by hand, in small batches, with high-quality ingredients and zero fillers. The oats are certified organic and gluten-free. It's the real scrumptious deal.

Crunchyology is all about that perfect bite: wholesome, delicious, and just a little bit addicting. Made with love, a dash of chaos, and a whole lot of crunch.
